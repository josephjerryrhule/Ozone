WHO WE ARE

Ozone is a proudly owned Ghanaian Internet / IT Solutions Provider established in September 2012. It is licensed and certified by the National Communications Authority (NCA) and the International Organization for Standardization (ISO) respectively.

Ozone offers robust and secure internet solutions across the country. Our goal is to provide superior internet service at very affordable prices to our cherished customers. Our portfolio ranges from highspeed internet to next-gen cloud-based services and other valued added services. We offer a wide range of services tailored to suit the specific and rapidly changing needs of our customers.

Our customer-centric approach to providing the service is what distinguishes us completely from others in the industry. Our use of the best technology available, a dynamic and energetic team combined with our 24x7x365 customer support contributes immensely to making us the customer's preferred choice. Because our priority is in customer satisfaction, we ensure our every effort is channeled into exceeding your expectations at every level.
